# Word Cloud Studio

Welcome! This brief introduction explains the vision for our interactive word
cloud website. The goal is to let anyone paste text, fine-tune the style, and
instantly visualize the most meaningful words.

## Key capabilities

- Paste content or upload a file to extract keywords.
- Choose palettes, fonts, and shapes to match any brand.
- Download high-res clouds for presentations or social posts.

## What’s next

Ready to start? Use the prototype canvas to experiment with layouts and help
shape the next generation of quick storytelling tools.
